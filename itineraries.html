Itineraries

3 Days in Paris

Day 1: Explore the Louvre and Notre-Dame.

Day 2: Visit the Eiffel Tower and Montmartre.

Day 3: Discover the Champs-Élysées and Luxembourg Gardens.

5 Days in New York

Day 1: Tour the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island.

Day 2: Explore Times Square and Broadway.

Day 3: Visit Central Park and The Met.

Day 4: Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge and explore Brooklyn.

Day 5: Enjoy a day at the American Museum of Natural History.

4 Days in Switzerland

Day 1: Zürich.

Day 2: Lucerne & Mt. Titlis.

Day 3: Visit Interlaken.

Day 4: Bern’s Old Town.

4 Days in Tokyo

Day 1: Visit modern Tokyo on the west side.

Day 2: Visit traditional Tokyo on the east side.

Day 3: A day trip outside of Tokyo to Nikko, Kamakura or Mt Fuji.

Day 4: Explore the Imperial Palace Area and Shinjuku.

6 Days in Italy

Day 1: Arrive in Venice, Private Guided City Tour.

Day 2: Bacaro & Private Squero Tour in Venice.

Day 3: Travel from Venice to Florence.

Day 4: Private Guided Accademia Gallery & Boat Tour.

Day 5: Free Day in Florence.

Day 6: Depart Florence.

4 Days in Turkey

Day 1: Istanbul.

Day 2: Visit Ankara.

Day 3: Explore Capadocia.

Day 4: A day in Safranbolu.
